Our Vission

To raise prayerful men and women who will reign over the nations and kingdoms, uprooting, pulling down, demolishing and overthrowing the works of darkness in order to establish the Kingdom of God thus fulfilling Gods will for His people.

Our Mission

Is to glorify God through Jesus Christ by making Disciples of all Nations through the continued Ministry of Jesus Christ in Worship, Evangelism, Discipleship, Deliverance, Fellowship and Mercy, Support and care for each otheras Gods family and encouraging personal Christian growth. (Jeremiah 1:10, Ephesians 4:11-16 and Luke 4:18-19).
