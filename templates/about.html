JESUS POWER CHRISTIAN MINISTRIES (JCPM)

Is an interdenominational member of the body of Christ affiliated to the Evangelical Fellowship of Zimbabwe (EFZ). Like EFZ we believe in the whole Bible and the Power and Working of the Holy Spirit.

OUR VALUES

Faith, Truth, Love, Righteousness, Holiness, Sanctification and Fellowship of Believers.

Our Mission

Is to glorify God through Jesus Christ by making Disciples of all Nations through the continued Ministry of Jesus Christ in Worship, Evangelism, Discipleship, Deliverance, Fellowship and Mercy, Support and care for each other as God’s family and encouraging personal Christian growth. (Jeremiah 1:10, Ephesians 4:11-16 and Luke 4:18-19).

Our Vission

To raise prayerful men and women who will reign over the nations and kingdoms, uprooting, pulling down, demolishing and overthrowing the works of darkness in order to establish the Kingdom of God thus fulfilling God’s will for His people.

Statement of Faith

  • We believe in the whole Bible and God’s plan to reconcile men to Himself through Jesus Christ and according to the Foundation and the teachings of Jesus Christ. We believe that the Bible is the inspired and infallible Word of God and that it is the answer to every human question or challenge you can ever think of. (2 Timothy 3:16 and 2 Peter 1:20-21).
  • We believe in the one and true God in the three Persons i.e. God the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit who is Omnipresent, Omniscient, and Omnipotent. (Ephesians 4:5-6, Matthew 3:16-17, 1 Corinthians 8:6 and Genesis 1:26, Revelations 19:6, Psalm 139:8-10, 1 John 5:6-7)
  • We believe in the Deity of Jesus Christ and that Jesus Christ was conceived of the Holy Spirit and born of a virgin. We also believe that Jesus died and rose from the dead in bodily form after three days, and that Jesus Christ ascended to heaven and that He is coming back a second time in the “Rapture” of the Church to judge the whole world. (John 1:1-4, Hebrews 1:1-5, Luke 1:30-35, Matthew 24:29-30, Acts 1:9-10, John 19:30-35, Luke 24:36-40, 1 Thessalonians 4:16-17.)
  • We believe that the only means of being cleansed from sin is repentance and faith in the precious Blood of Jesus. (Acts 3:19, Luke 24:47, Ephesians 1:7, 1 John 1:7-9)
  • We believe that Jesus Christ came to this world to set captives free, and in the five-fold Ministries given to some by Jesus Christ. (Luke 4:18-19, Ephesians 4:11).
  • We believe that regeneration by the Holy Spirit is essential for personal salvation through Jesus Christ. (Titus 3:5, John 3:3-5.).
  • We believe that Jesus Christ is the mediator between God and man, and that the finished work on the cross provides divine healing for the body and salvation of the soul for everyone that believes. (1 Peter 2:24, 1 Timothy 2:4-5, Acts 3:16 and Acts 9:32-35).
  • We believe that quality service to God does not come by might or power but by the Holy Spirit. We believe that when an individual receives the Holy Spirit, he/she receives the divine enablement for Christian service and witness. (Acts 1:8, Acts 2:4, Acts 3:1-26 and Acts 4:5-12).
  • We believe in the final resurrection and judgment of both the saved and the unsaved, both the dead and the living. We also believe in eternal life for the saved and eternal judgment for the unsaved. (Revelation 20:11-15, 1 Corinthians 15:12-23).
  • We believe that Christ is the head of the Church and that the Church should make known to the world the finished work of Christ Jesus and the power in that Name. (Colossians 1:17-18, Ephesians 3:10, Philippians 2:9-11, Romans 15:16).

SPECIFIC CALLING

Though JPCM has the general mission as all other members of the body of Christ to reach the lost with the Gospel of Christ; it has a specific calling into deliverance ministry guided by Jeremiah 1:10. Certain long- standing problems in life such as poverty (both spiritual and physical) generational problems, can only be addressed through deliverance which can go to the root source of the problem and arrest it once and for all. After deliverance it is critical for a person to remain free through enhanced spiritual growth.
